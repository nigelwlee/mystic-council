# Tarot Spread Positions

## Single Card
- **Card 1**: The core message or current energy. What you most need to know right now.

## Three-Card Spread (Past / Present / Future)
- **Card 1 (Past)**: What has led to this moment. Influences from the recent or distant past.
- **Card 2 (Present)**: The current situation, energy, or challenge at hand.
- **Card 3 (Future)**: Where current energies are flowing. The likely outcome if things continue as they are.

## Five-Card Spread
- **Card 1**: The present situation — the heart of the matter.
- **Card 2**: The challenge or obstacle to be aware of.
- **Card 3**: The past influence shaping the present.
- **Card 4**: The future outcome if current energies continue.
- **Card 5**: Advice — what to focus on or the hidden factor.

## Reading Principles
- Cards don't predict a fixed fate — they show current energies and tendencies.
- Reversed cards indicate blocked, internalized, or shadow expression of the card's energy.
- The relationship between cards is often more meaningful than any single card.
- Court cards may represent people in the querent's life, or aspects of themselves.
- The final card often carries the most important message.
