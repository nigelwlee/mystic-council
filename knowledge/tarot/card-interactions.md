# Tarot Card Interactions and Reading Techniques

## Suit Energies
- **Wands (Fire)**: Passion, inspiration, career, creative energy, action
- **Cups (Water)**: Emotions, relationships, intuition, the subconscious
- **Swords (Air)**: Mind, intellect, conflict, communication, truth
- **Pentacles (Earth)**: Material world, money, body, work, nature

## When Suits Dominate a Spread
- Mostly Wands: A time of action, ambition, career movement
- Mostly Cups: Emotional themes, relationship focus, inner work
- Mostly Swords: Mental stress, decisions, conflict, important communications
- Mostly Pentacles: Practical matters, financial concerns, physical health
- Mixed suits: Complex situation with multiple life areas in play

## Major vs. Minor Arcana
- **Many Major Arcana**: Significant life events, fate at work, deep soul lessons
- **Mostly Minor Arcana**: Everyday practical matters, more within personal control

## Key Card Combinations
- **The Tower + Death**: Profound transformation — the old structure must fully collapse
- **The Star after The Tower**: After crisis comes renewal and hope
- **The Lovers + Two of Cups**: Strong relationship energy, partnership theme
- **Ten of Pentacles + Four of Wands**: Lasting prosperity and domestic harmony
- **Three of Swords + The Moon**: Hidden grief, illusion causing emotional pain
- **Wheel of Fortune + The World**: A major cycle completing with positive outcome
- **The Hermit + Four of Cups**: Deep withdrawal and contemplation needed
- **Strength + King/Queen of any suit**: Mastery through inner work

## Reversed Cards
Reversed cards can indicate: blocked energy, internalized themes, delays, shadow expression, need to look within rather than without. Context determines which applies.
