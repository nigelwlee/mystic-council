# The 27 Nakshatras

Each nakshatra spans 13°20' of the sidereal zodiac. The Moon's nakshatra at birth is central to Vedic interpretation.

| # | Name | Ruler | Symbol | Theme |
|---|------|-------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Ashwini | Ketu | Horse head | Healing, speed, beginnings |
| 2 | Bharani | Venus | Yoni | Transformation, carrying life and death |
| 3 | Krittika | Sun | Razor/flame | Purification, determination, fire |
| 4 | Rohini | Moon | Cart/chariot | Fertility, beauty, growth |
| 5 | Mrigashira | Mars | Deer head | Seeking, curiosity, gentleness |
| 6 | Ardra | Rahu | Teardrop | Storms, transformation, Rudra's power |
| 7 | Punarvasu | Jupiter | Quiver of arrows | Renewal, return, expansion |
| 8 | Pushya | Saturn | Flower/cow udder | Nourishment, wisdom, dharma |
| 9 | Ashlesha | Mercury | Coiled serpent | Penetrating insight, kundalini, mysticism |
| 10 | Magha | Ketu | Palanquin | Ancestral power, throne, legacy |
| 11 | Purva Phalguni | Venus | Hammock/front legs | Rest, pleasure, creative release |
| 12 | Uttara Phalguni | Sun | Bed/back legs | Partnership, support, patronage |
| 13 | Hasta | Moon | Hand | Skill, craft, healing hands |
| 14 | Chitra | Mars | Pearl/bright jewel | Beauty, artistry, architecture |
| 15 | Swati | Rahu | Sword/coral | Independence, flexibility, wind |
| 16 | Vishakha | Jupiter | Triumphal arch | Goal-oriented, patience, purpose |
| 17 | Anuradha | Saturn | Lotus | Devotion, friendship, success abroad |

## Nakshatra Qualities (Guna)
- **Deva** (divine): Ashwini, Mrigashira, Punarvasu, Pushya, Hasta, Swati, Anuradha, Shravana, Revati
- **Manushya** (human): Bharani, Rohini, Ardra, Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni, Purva Ashadha, Uttara Ashadha, Purva Bhadrapada, Uttara Bhadrapada
- **Rakshasa** (demonic/intense): Krittika, Ashlesha, Magha, Chitra, Vishakha, Jyeshtha, Mula, Dhanishtha, Shatabhisha
