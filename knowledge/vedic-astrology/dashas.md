# Vimshottari Dasha System

The Vimshottari Dasha is a 120-year planetary period system central to Vedic predictive astrology. The Moon's nakshatra at birth determines which dasha begins at birth and for how long.

## Dasha Sequence and Duration
| Planet | Years | Keywords |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Ketu | 7 | Spirituality, past karma, detachment, sudden events |
| Venus | 20 | Relationships, pleasure, arts, material comfort |
| Sun | 6 | Father, authority, vitality, career, recognition |
| Moon | 10 | Mother, emotions, mind, public, travel |
| Mars | 7 | Action, energy, siblings, property, conflicts |
| Rahu | 18 | Foreign things, ambition, illusion, sudden rise/fall |
| Jupiter | 16 | Wisdom, expansion, dharma, children, teachers |
| Saturn | 19 | Discipline, delays, hard work, karma, longevity |
| Mercury | 17 | Communication, intellect, business, education, siblings |

Total cycle: 120 years

## How to Calculate
1. Find the Moon's nakshatra at birth
2. Determine how far the Moon is through that nakshatra (fractional position)
3. Calculate remaining years in the birth dasha
4. Sequence forward through the dasha order

## Interpretation Principles
- The dasha ruler planet's strength in the natal chart affects dasha outcomes
- Planets in their own sign, exaltation, or ruling favorable houses give better results
- Sub-periods (Antardashas) within each Mahadasha refine the timing further
- Transits of the dasha ruler over natal planets trigger specific events

## Favorable vs. Unfavorable Dashas
- Generally positive: Jupiter, Venus, Mercury (when well-placed)
- Mixed: Moon, Sun, Mars (depend heavily on chart position)
- Commonly challenging: Saturn, Rahu, Ketu (but can give spiritual gifts or sudden gains)
