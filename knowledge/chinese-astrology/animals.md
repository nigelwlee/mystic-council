# Chinese Zodiac Animals

**Rat** (子, Zǐ) — Quick-witted, resourceful, versatile, adaptable. Charming but opportunistic. Best with: Dragon, Monkey, Ox.
**Ox** (丑, Chǒu) — Diligent, dependable, strong, patient. Stubborn but reliable. Best with: Rat, Snake, Rooster.
**Tiger** (寅, Yín) — Brave, competitive, unpredictable, charming. Natural leaders. Best with: Dragon, Horse, Pig.
**Rabbit** (卯, Mǎo) — Gentle, elegant, kind, quiet. Sensitive and artistic. Best with: Goat, Pig, Dog.
**Dragon** (辰, Chén) — Energetic, fearless, warm-hearted, charismatic. The most auspicious sign. Best with: Rat, Tiger, Monkey.
**Snake** (巳, Sì) — Deep thinker, intuitive, elegant, attentive. Mysterious and wise. Best with: Dragon, Rooster, Ox.
**Horse** (午, Wǔ) — Energetic, independent, impatient, travel-loving. Free spirits. Best with: Tiger, Goat, Dog.
**Goat** (未, Wèi) — Creative, gentle, compassionate, shy. Artistic and peace-loving. Best with: Horse, Rabbit, Pig.
**Monkey** (申, Shēn) — Clever, curious, witty, mischievous. Quick learners. Best with: Rat, Dragon, Snake.
**Rooster** (酉, Yǒu) — Observant, hardworking, courageous, direct. Honest to a fault. Best with: Ox, Dragon, Snake.
**Dog** (戌, Xū) — Loyal, honest, kind, anxious. Trustworthy companions. Best with: Rabbit, Tiger, Horse.
**Pig** (亥, Hài) — Diligent, compassionate, generous, indulgent. Sincere and warm. Best with: Tiger, Rabbit, Goat.

## Fixed Elements per Sign
- Rat: Water | Ox: Earth | Tiger: Wood | Rabbit: Wood | Dragon: Earth | Snake: Fire
- Horse: Fire | Goat: Earth | Monkey: Metal | Rooster: Metal | Dog: Earth | Pig: Water
