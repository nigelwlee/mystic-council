# Planetary Aspects

**Conjunction (0°, orb 8°)** — Planets merge their energies. Intense focus. Can be harmonious or challenging depending on planets involved.
**Sextile (60°, orb 6°)** — Easy flowing opportunity. Talents and opportunities that require some action to activate.
**Square (90°, orb 8°)** — Tension and challenge driving action. Friction that motivates growth through conflict.
**Trine (120°, orb 8°)** — Natural harmony and ease. Talents that flow effortlessly, though can lead to complacency.
**Opposition (180°, orb 8°)** — Polarity and tension between two forces seeking balance. Often plays out through relationships.

## Key Aspect Combinations
- Sun-Moon: Ego and emotion in sync (conjunction) or tension (square/opposition)
- Sun-Saturn: Discipline shapes identity; potential for both achievement and restriction
- Venus-Mars: Romantic and sexual tension; love meets desire
- Jupiter-Saturn: Expansion vs. contraction; the great rhythm of growth and consolidation
- Mercury-Neptune: Inspiration meets confusion; intuitive communication or deception
