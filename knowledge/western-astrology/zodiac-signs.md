# Zodiac Signs

**Aries** (Mar 21–Apr 19) — Fire, Cardinal. Ruled by Mars. Pioneering, courageous, impulsive, independent. The initiator.
**Taurus** (Apr 20–May 20) — Earth, Fixed. Ruled by Venus. Stable, sensual, patient, stubborn, love of beauty and security.
**Gemini** (May 21–Jun 20) — Air, Mutable. Ruled by Mercury. Curious, communicative, adaptable, dual-natured, witty.
**Cancer** (Jun 21–Jul 22) — Water, Cardinal. Ruled by Moon. Nurturing, intuitive, protective, emotional, home-oriented.
**Leo** (Jul 23–Aug 22) — Fire, Fixed. Ruled by Sun. Charismatic, generous, dramatic, proud, creative, leadership.
**Virgo** (Aug 23–Sep 22) — Earth, Mutable. Ruled by Mercury. Analytical, practical, perfectionistic, service-oriented.
**Libra** (Sep 23–Oct 22) — Air, Cardinal. Ruled by Venus. Diplomatic, aesthetic, relationship-focused, seeks justice and balance.
**Scorpio** (Oct 23–Nov 21) — Water, Fixed. Ruled by Mars/Pluto. Intense, secretive, transformative, powerful, psychic.
**Sagittarius** (Nov 22–Dec 21) — Fire, Mutable. Ruled by Jupiter. Philosophical, adventurous, optimistic, honest, freedom-loving.
**Capricorn** (Dec 22–Jan 19) — Earth, Cardinal. Ruled by Saturn. Ambitious, disciplined, traditional, responsible, pragmatic.
**Aquarius** (Jan 20–Feb 18) — Air, Fixed. Ruled by Saturn/Uranus. Humanitarian, innovative, detached, progressive, rebellious.
**Pisces** (Feb 19–Mar 20) — Water, Mutable. Ruled by Jupiter/Neptune. Empathic, dreamy, spiritual, compassionate, fluid.

## Elements
- **Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): passionate, inspired, dynamic
- **Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): practical, grounded, material
- **Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): intellectual, social, communicative
- **Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): emotional, intuitive, psychic

## Modalities
- **Cardinal** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): initiating, leadership
- **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): sustaining, determined, stubborn
- **Mutable** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): adapting, flexible, transitional
