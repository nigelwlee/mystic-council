# Planets in Western Astrology

**Sun** — Core identity, ego, vitality, life purpose. The conscious self.
**Moon** — Emotions, instincts, subconscious, nurturing, the past, home.
**Mercury** — Mind, communication, intellect, learning, short travel.
**Venus** — Love, beauty, pleasure, values, aesthetics, money.
**Mars** — Drive, action, aggression, passion, courage, sexuality.
**Jupiter** — Expansion, luck, wisdom, philosophy, abundance, growth.
**Saturn** — Discipline, restriction, karma, structure, responsibility, time.
**Uranus** — Revolution, innovation, awakening, disruption, originality.
**Neptune** — Dreams, illusion, spirituality, dissolution, mysticism.
**Pluto** — Transformation, power, death/rebirth, the unconscious depths.

## Dignities
- Sun rules Leo; exalted in Aries
- Moon rules Cancer; exalted in Taurus
- Mercury rules Gemini/Virgo; exalted in Virgo
- Venus rules Taurus/Libra; exalted in Pisces
- Mars rules Aries/Scorpio; exalted in Capricorn
- Jupiter rules Sagittarius/Pisces; exalted in Cancer
- Saturn rules Capricorn/Aquarius; exalted in Libra
