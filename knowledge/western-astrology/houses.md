# The 12 Houses

1st House (Ascendant) — Self, appearance, first impressions, identity, physical body.
2nd House — Money, possessions, values, self-worth, personal resources.
3rd House — Communication, siblings, short travel, local environment, early education.
4th House (IC) — Home, family, roots, mother, private life, emotional foundation.
5th House — Creativity, romance, children, play, self-expression, pleasure.
6th House — Health, daily routine, work, service, pets, wellness habits.
7th House (Descendant) — Partnerships, marriage, open enemies, one-on-one relationships.
8th House — Transformation, shared resources, death/rebirth, sexuality, occult, inheritance.
9th House — Philosophy, higher education, long travel, religion, beliefs, law.
10th House (MC/Midheaven) — Career, public reputation, father, authority, life ambition.
11th House — Friends, groups, community, hopes and wishes, social causes.
12th House — Hidden things, isolation, spirituality, karma, self-undoing, the unconscious.
