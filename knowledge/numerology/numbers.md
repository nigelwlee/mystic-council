# Core Number Meanings

## Life Path Numbers
The most important number — derived from your birth date. Describes your life's journey, key lessons, and innate traits.

**1** — The Leader. Independence, originality, ambition, pioneering spirit. Here to forge new paths.
**2** — The Diplomat. Cooperation, sensitivity, balance, intuition. Here to create harmony and partnership.
**3** — The Communicator. Creativity, self-expression, joy, optimism. Here to inspire and entertain.
**4** — The Builder. Stability, discipline, hard work, practicality. Here to create lasting foundations.
**5** — The Adventurer. Freedom, change, curiosity, versatility. Here to experience life fully.
**6** — The Nurturer. Responsibility, care, family, service, beauty. Here to love and protect.
**7** — The Seeker. Analysis, introspection, spirituality, wisdom. Here to seek truth and understanding.
**8** — The Achiever. Power, ambition, material success, karma. Here to master the material world.
**9** — The Humanitarian. Compassion, completion, wisdom, universal love. Here to serve the greater good.

## Expression (Destiny) Numbers
Derived from your full birth name. Reveals your natural talents, abilities, and the way your life tends to unfold.

## Soul Urge Numbers  
Derived from vowels in your name. Reveals your deepest desires, inner motivations, and what your soul longs for.

## Personality Numbers
Derived from consonants in your name. Reveals how others perceive you — your outer personality.
